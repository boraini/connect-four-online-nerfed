How to Play

When it is your turn, click a lane to “drop” a canoe into it. Try to arrange four canoes horizontally, vertically, or diagonally as seen on the left before your opponent, or before the lanes fill up!

The background, a photo of the Abtwoudsebrug in Delft, is a courtesy of M. Minderhoud on the Wikimedia Commons. Licensed CC-BY-SA 3.0.
